ABOUT US

Welcome to TRAVELANKA, your trusted guide to exploring the beauty and culture of Sri Lanka. This site is made to help travelers discover the island’s charm through its beaches, mountains, wildlife, and historical landmarks. Whether you are a backpacker, an international tourist, or a local traveler looking for new experiences, TRAVELANKA will help you find the best places to visit and the most enjoyable ways to experience them. Our goal is to make your journey across Sri Lanka effortless, inspiring, and full of meaningful experiences. We bring together all the essential travel information in one place so that you can easily plan your trip based on your interests, preferences, and budget. From sun-kissed beaches to misty mountains, from ancient temples to modern cities, Sri Lanka offers endless possibilities. TRAVELANKA is your trusted companion in exploring them all.

I’m Anasinghe, and I’ve been passionate about exploring Sri Lanka for as long as I can remember. Over the years, I’ve traveled extensively across this beautiful island and I’ve always dreamed of helping others experience Sri Lanka in a way that’s authentic, memorable, and uniquely theirs. That’s why I started Travelanka in 2015.

Since then, we’ve grown into a dedicated team of travel enthusiasts, guides, and local experts who share my love for this country. Based in Galle, we’ve spent years crafting experiences that go beyond sightseeing, connecting visitors with the people, traditions, and hidden gems that make Sri Lanka truly special.

Our mission is simple to make travel personal and meaningful. We believe that exploring Sri Lanka should be more than just sightseeing. It should be a connection with people traditions and places that make this land truly unique. Every tour we design focuses on real experiences that create lasting memories.

At Travelanka we take pride in offering experiences that go beyond ordinary vacations. Whether you are searching for hidden waterfalls ancient temples or wildlife adventures we make sure your journey is smooth comfortable and unforgettable. Our local expertise allows us to bring you closer to the heart of Sri Lanka in ways you will always remember.

We work closely with local communities to ensure our travels support the people who make this country special. Every guide driver and host we collaborate with shares our love for Sri Lanka and our respect for its environment. When you travel with us you help preserve the natural and cultural heritage of this island for future generations.

Our team is built on trust passion and experience. We have explored every region from the bustling streets of Colombo to the peaceful tea gardens of Nuwara Eliya. This knowledge helps us craft journeys that reflect the real spirit of Sri Lanka. With us you do not just visit a place you live it.

Travelanka is more than a travel service. It is your companion in adventure your storyteller and your bridge to authentic Sri Lankan life. We invite you to join us on a journey filled with discovering joy and the beauty of simplicity. Come experience Sri Lanka your way with Travelanka.
